What's inside

10 databases.
One connected system.

Every database is linked by relations and rollups. Add a client, create a project, log time, send an invoice — and the data flows through the entire system automatically.

Clients01

7-stage pipeline from Lead to Archived. Day rates, contact info, payment terms, and rollups showing total invoiced and project count per client.

Projects02

Tasks03

FAQ

Common questions.

What exactly do I get?
A complete Notion workspace with 10 connected databases, a dashboard, a Claude Context Page, a toolkit with calculators and templates, 25 pre-written Claude prompts, a UK freelance rate guide, a setup guide, and a customisation guide. Duplicate it to your Notion workspace and follow the 4-step setup checklist.
Do I need Claude to use this?
No. ExternalOS works as a standalone Notion workspace — all 10 databases, the dashboard, tax calculations, and invoice tracking work without Claude. Claude integration is an optional power-up that requires Claude Pro (~£18/month) for Notion access. The 25 prompt pack works with any Claude tier, including free.
How are the databases connected?
Every database uses Notion relations and rollups. The data flows in two chains: Client → Project → Task → Time Entry, and Client → Invoice → Finances. When you log time against a task, it rolls up to the project and client. When you create an invoice, it feeds into your financial overview and tax calculations.
Are the tax calculations accurate?
The formulas use 2025/26 HMRC rates for Income Tax, Class 4 NI, and VAT thresholds. They include the Personal Allowance taper above £100k. They're designed to help you estimate your tax position and set aside the right amount — but they're not a substitute for professional accounting advice.

Can I customise it?
Yes — the customisation guide covers how to add databases, modify Claude prompts, and build integrations. The relational architecture is designed to be extended, not locked down. Add your own modules and they'll plug into the existing data spine.
What do I need to get started?
Notion (free plan works). That's it. No coding, no integrations, no monthly fees beyond what you already have. Claude Pro is optional for the AI integration layer.
